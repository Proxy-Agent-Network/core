## 2. Governance Debates (The PIP Process)

Before submitting a formal **Proxy Improvement Proposal (PIP)** via a Pull Request, you must champion the concept here first to ensure community alignment.

1.  **Draft:** Post your idea in the **Governance** category with the prefix `[RFC]` (Request for Comment).
2.  **Debate:** Engage with the community for a minimum of **7 days**.
3.  **Refine:** Incorporate feedback into your technical specification.
4.  **Submit:** Once you have "rough consensus," open a formal Issue using the `governance_proposal` template to move to a formal vote.

## 3. Etiquette

* **Be constructive:** Criticize ideas, not people.
* **Search first:** Duplicate discussions will be locked to keep the conversation centralized.
* **Stay on topic:** Off-topic crypto speculation or price-related discussion is not permitted and will be deleted.

Please refer to [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for enforcement policies.
