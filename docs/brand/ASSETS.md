# **Proxy Agent Network (PAN) | Brand & Fleet Assets**

**Status:** Active (Mesa Pilot)

**Version:** 2026.1.0

**Target:** Vanguard Agents, Marketing, & Fleet Partners

## **1\. Brand Philosophy: "Tactical Infrastructure"**

The Proxy Agent Network (PAN) is not a consumer app, nor is it a private security firm. PAN is a decentralized physical infrastructure network.

Our brand must project **authority, technical precision, and safety**. When a Vanguard Agent approaches a stranded $150,000 autonomous vehicle in a busy intersection, both the remote Fleet Operators and the surrounding civilian public must immediately recognize the Agent as an official, authorized infrastructure technician.

## **2\. Core Color Palette**

PAN utilizes a high-contrast, dual-purpose palette that bridges the digital network layer with the physical, arid environment of the Mesa Operations Sector.

### **Primary Colors**

* **Infrastructure Slate (Navy):** \#0A192F \- Used for primary backgrounds, uniform polos, and digital dark-mode interfaces. Projects stability and enterprise trust.  
* **Arizona Copper:** \#B87333 \- Our primary accent. Represents our Mesa/Maricopa County roots and the physical wiring/hardware of the DePIN network.

### **Action & Digital Colors**

* **LiDAR Cyan:** \#64FFDA \- Used strictly for digital success states, UWB lock confirmations, and tactical HUD elements.  
* **L402 Lightning (Blue):** \#007BFF \- Used for financial transactions and Lightning Network settlement icons.

### **Safety (Physical Only)**

* **MUTCD High-Vis Yellow:** \#E8FF00 \- Restricted to physical Vanguard Agent PPE (Class 3 vests) and response vehicle hazard markings to comply with Department of Transportation safety standards.

## **3\. Typography**

* **Primary Font (Headings & UI):** Urbanist (Sans-Serif). Clean, modern, and highly legible on mobile devices under harsh Arizona sunlight.  
* **Secondary Font (Data & Telemetry):** JetBrains Mono (Monospace). Used exclusively in the Tactical App and Fleet Dashboards for displaying GPS coordinates, L402 Satoshi balances, and UDS Fault Codes.

## **4\. Vanguard Agent Uniforms (PPE)**

Agents must adhere to strict uniform standards while on ACTIVE\_PATROL to maintain the $5M HNOA liability shield.

### **The "On-Site" Loadout**

* **Base Layer:** PAN-issued "Infrastructure Slate" moisture-wicking tactical polo.  
* **Left Chest:** Embroidered PAN primary logo (Copper/Slate).  
* **Right Sleeve:** Subdued American Flag patch (forward-facing).  
* **Outerwear (Mandatory on roadways):** ANSI/ISEA 107-2020 Class 3 High-Visibility Safety Vest. PAN logo printed on the rear shoulder panel.  
* **Identification:** PAN physical cryptographic ID badge worn on a breakaway lanyard or chest clip.

## **5\. Response Vehicle Asset Markings**

Vanguard Agents utilize their personal vehicles for macro-routing. However, during an active ORP (Optical Reclamation Protocol) intervention, the vehicle must be temporarily marked as an official response asset.

* **Magnetic Door Decals:** 24" x 12" high-reflectivity magnetic signs.  
  * *Text:* "PROXY AGENT NETWORK \- AUTONOMOUS RECOVERY UNIT"  
  * *Identifier:* Agent's unique 4-digit Vanguard ID printed in high-contrast cyan.  
* **Warning Lights:** Magnet-mounted Amber/White LED hazard light bar (SAE J845 Class 1 certified). *Red and Blue lights are strictly prohibited by law.*

## **6\. Digital Logo Assets**

For digital integrations, Fleet Partners may utilize the PAN logos inside their internal dispatch dashboards.

* **Primary Mark:** The "Hex-Shield" (A geometric hexagon containing a stylized routing node and copper wiring motif).  
* **Wordmark:** "PROXY AGENT NETWORK" set in Urbanist Bold, with "NETWORK" offset in Arizona Copper.  
* **Dark Mode Preferred:** PAN logos should default to dark mode backgrounds (\#0A192F or \#020617) whenever possible to align with our digital Tactical HUD aesthetic.  
* **Clear Space:** Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to 50% of the Hex-Shield's height.
