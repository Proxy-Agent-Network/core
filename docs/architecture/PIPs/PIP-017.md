## **1\. Abstract**

This proposal codifies the trustless, zero-custody payment mechanism for the Proxy Agent Network (PAN). It replaces legacy 30-day net invoicing with **L402 HODL Invoices**, establishing a direct Machine-to-Human (M2H) economic settlement layer. Settlement of the Satoshi bounty is strictly contingent upon the Autonomous Vehicle's (AV) onboard AI cryptographically verifying the physical hardware resolution and releasing the preimage.

## **2\. Motivation**

Traditional gig-economy platforms hold user funds in centralized escrow, creating massive regulatory and custodial liabilities. Furthermore, human-in-the-loop dispute resolution (e.g., an Agent claiming they cleaned a LiDAR dome while the Fleet Operator claims it is still dirty) is too slow for real-time AV routing. By utilizing L402 HTLCs (Hashed Timelock Contracts), PAN removes itself as a financial custodian and makes the AV the ultimate, deterministic arbiter of task success.

## **3\. Technical Specifications**

* **Settlement Layer:** L402 standard built atop the Bitcoin Lightning Network (LND/LDK).  
* **Escrow State (HELD):** When a Fleet Operator dispatches a Vanguard Agent, the Satoshi bounty is routed to the PAN Gateway. The routing node mathematically locks the funds in a HELD state. The funds have left the Fleet Treasury but have not credited the Agent's wallet.  
* **Settlement Trigger (The Preimage):** The 32-byte cryptographic preimage (the secret required to claim the funds) is held by the PAN Gateway. It is **only** released to the Agent's mobile wallet when the Gateway receives the FAULT\_CLEARED UDS webhook from the AV, confirming the Optical Reclamation Protocol (ORP) was successful.

## **4\. Timeouts & Financial Slashing**

To prevent Fleet liquidity from being permanently locked by unresponsive Agents or unsolvable hardware faults, the escrow is strictly time-bounded:

1. **The 15-Minute SLA (Routing Cancellation):** If the assigned Vanguard Agent fails to achieve a TPM-signed UWB proximity lock within 15 minutes of accepting the contract, the PAN Gateway instantly cancels the HODL invoice. Funds revert to the Fleet Treasury, and the Agent suffers a \-15 VTS Reputation Slash.  
2. **The 30-Minute Hard Timeout (Hardware Failure):** If the Agent completes the ORP, but the AV's sensor array is permanently damaged and fails to send the FAULT\_CLEARED signal, the HTLC reaches its absolute network timeout (30 minutes). The invoice is canceled, funds revert to the Fleet, and the mission is escalated for manual Fleet tow retrieval.

## **5\. Economic & Legal Impact**

This architectural standard completely eliminates the risk of "Phantom Agent" fraud. A Vanguard Agent cannot spoof their location or falsely claim a bounty without the autonomous vehicle physically verifying the work. Concurrently, PAN avoids all money transmission licenses (MTL) and banking regulations by never taking legal custody of Fleet capital.
