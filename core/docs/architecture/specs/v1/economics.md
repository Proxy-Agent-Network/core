# **Proxy Agent Network (PAN) | Network Economics & Fee Schedule**

**Status:** Active (Mesa Pilot)

**Version:** 2026.1.0

**Unit of Account:** Bitcoin Satoshi (Sats)

**Target:** Fleet Finance Teams & PAN Operations

## **1\. Zero-Custody Settlement (L402)**

The Proxy Agent Network (PAN) operates as a pure physical routing and compliance layer. To avoid Money Transmission License (MTL) requirements and protect Fleet capital, PAN utilizes a **Zero-Custody** financial architecture via the Lightning Network (L402).

* **No Fleet Deposits:** Fleet Operators do not pre-fund a central PAN corporate account.  
* **No Agent Wallets:** PAN does not custody Vanguard Agent earnings.  
* **Point-to-Point:** Capital moves directly from the Fleet Operator's Lightning Node to the Vanguard Agent's mobile Lightning wallet via cryptographically locked Hashed Timelock Contracts (HTLCs).

## **2\. USD Denomination & Sat Settlement**

While the physical world and our Fleet Partners measure OpEx in US Dollars (USD), the underlying protocol executes strictly in Satoshis to enable sub-second, programmatic settlement.

To protect Vanguard Agents from inter-minute price volatility during a mission:

1. **The Oracle Sync:** The PAN Gateway syncs a Time-Weighted Average Price (TWAP) from institutional index feeds every 60 seconds.  
2. **The L402 Lock:** When an Agent taps ACCEPT on a mission, the exact Satoshi amount is locked into the HTLC.  
3. **Volatility Insulation:** The Vanguard Agent is guaranteed the exact Satoshi amount displayed at the time of acceptance, regardless of fiat volatility during the 15-minute Service Level Agreement (SLA) window.

## **3\. The Infrastructure Routing Fee**

Vanguard Agents receive **100%** of the advertised L402 Satoshi bounty. PAN does not extract a percentage from the worker's earnings.

Instead, PAN charges a **Routing & Compliance Fee** directly to the Fleet Operator. This fee is added to the total L402 invoice generated by the PAN Gateway.

| Fee Component | Amount | Value Provided to Fleet Operator |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Agent Bounty** | Base (e.g., $15.00) \* Surge | 100% passed to the Vanguard Agent for physical execution. |
| **Routing Fee** | \+ 15% of Bounty | Sector dispatch, UWB homing servers, and L402 HTLC generation. |
| **Liability Shield** | \+ $2.50 (Flat) | Coverage under PAN's $5,000,000 HNOA and Tech E\&O insurance policy. |
| **SB 1417 Audit** | \+ $1.00 (Flat) | Generation and 7-year WORM storage of the TPM-signed Optical Health Report. |

*Example: A standard $15.00 ORP dispatch will cost the Fleet Operator a total of $20.75 (settled in Sats). This represents a \~91% OpEx reduction compared to dispatching a $240 legacy Fleet Chase Van.*

## **4\. The Demise of "Worker Staking"**

Legacy decentralized networks often require human nodes to lock up financial collateral (staking) to participate.

PAN completely rejects the financial staking model. Requiring Veterans to purchase and lock up Bitcoin to work creates unnecessary friction and resembles predatory multi-level marketing structures.

### **The Cyber-Physical "Stake"**

Vanguard Agents do not post financial bonds. Their "stake" in the network is physical and reputational:

1. **Hardware Capital:** The Agent must possess a whitelisted PAN-certified mobile device (iPhone 16 Pro+ / Pixel 9 Pro+), representing a \~$1,000 personal hardware investment.  
2. **Time & Fuel:** The Agent expends personal fuel and time to navigate to the stranded Autonomous Vehicle.  
3. **The Vanguard Trust Score (VTS):** If an Agent fails to clear the fault or breaches the SLA, the L402 escrow reverts to the Fleet Operator. The Agent loses their expended time/fuel, receives $0, and suffers a VTS slashing penalty that jeopardizes their future earning potential.

This creates perfect alignment of incentives without requiring Vanguard Agents to custody idle capital.

## **5\. Fleet Liquidity Requirements (Treasury)**

Because Fleet Operators are the financial principals in the M2H economy, they must maintain highly liquid Lightning Nodes.

To maintain their **Concurrent Mission Quotas** (as defined in sdk/quotas.md), Fleet Partners must provision sufficient outbound channel liquidity to support their maximum theoretical surge spend. If a Fleet Node lacks the liquidity to route a requested L402 HODL invoice, the PAN Gateway will return a 402 Payment Required (L402\_ESCROW\_FAILED) error, and the Vanguard Agent will not be dispatched.
