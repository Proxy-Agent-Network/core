# PROXY NODE SERVICE AGREEMENT (v1)

**Effective Date:** Upon Cryptographic Stake Lock  
**Between:** Proxy Protocol Network ("Network") **AND** The Authenticated Human Node ("Proxy")

---

### 1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
The Proxy acknowledges that they are an independent contractor and not an employee of the Network or the AI Agents they serve. The Proxy is solely responsible for all taxes, insurance, and benefits.

### 2. CONFIDENTIALITY & DATA PRIVACY (The "Zero-Knowledge" Clause)
The Proxy agrees to:

* **A.** Treat all data received from an Agent (SMS codes, document contents, visual data) as **Strictly Confidential**.
* **B.** Destroy all local copies of Agent data immediately upon task completion.
* **C.** Never attempt to reverse-engineer the identity of the Principal (the human owner of the AI Agent).

> **Warning:** Violation of this clause results in immediate forfeiture of Staked Funds and permanent ban from the Network.

### 3. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
By accepting a task, the Proxy agrees to execute it within the time window specified in the `task_timer`. Failure to complete the task within the window will result in a negative reputation score.

### 4. PROHIBITED ACTIONS
The Proxy shall **not**:

1.  Use the Agent's authority to transfer funds to themselves.
2.  Sign documents that are clearly illegal or fraudulent.
3.  Sub-contract the task to an unverified third party.

### 5. PAYMENT & ESCROW
Payment is released programmatically via the **Lightning Network** upon valid proof of work (e.g., webhook confirmation). All payment disputes are subject to the Network's decentralized jury process.
